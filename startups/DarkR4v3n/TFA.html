The Fate of Atlantis

STORY ARC 2 – THE FATE OF ATLANTIS

Venture into the fabled realms of Greek mythology to discover your hero's true power and unravel the mysteries of the First Civilization. Explore three new worlds from Greek mythology – Elysium, the Underworld, and Atlantis! Face legendary trials and encounter the gods themselves, including Persephone, Hades, Poseidon, and more! Delve deeper into Assassin's Creed lore and uncover the mysterious power of the First Civilization.

The Fate of Atlantis is the second post-launch story arc of Assassin's Creed® Odyssey, which includes 3 new episodic stories. Players can dive into new episodes as they premiere, or experience the epic adventures in their entirety once all episodes are available.

Available now

Episode 3: Judgment of Atlantis

Journey into a fabled city of legend, encounter the mighty Poseidon, and discover the realm's destiny in the final episode of The Fate of Atlantis!

The third and final episode is downloadable for players who already own the Season Pass or Gold Edition

Episode 2: Torment of Hades

In "Torment of Hades", the second episode of The Fate of Atlantis, players will go beyond the gates of the Underworld to help the lost souls find peace and face Hades himself.

The second episode is downloadable for players who already own the Season Pass or Gold Edition, the third episode will complete the second story arc.

Episode 1: Fields of Elysium

In "Fields of Elysium", the first episode of The Fate of Atlantis, players will explore the Greek afterlife in the paradise of Elysium and discover what sinister secrets are hiding behind its beautiful façade.

The first episode is downloadable for players who already own the Season Pass or Gold Edition, the second and third episodes will complete the second story arc.
